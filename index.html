Who We Are

Cred is a credibility intelligence application. We measure the accuracy of influencers, politicians, and public figures so you know who to trust.

Our app turns statements, posts, and clips into transparent credibility scores backed by reputable sources. We make fact-checking accessible, digestible, and engaging — helping society cut through misinformation and see which voices are truly reliable.

Capture

We collect statements from influencers, politicians, and public figures — everything from tweets to speeches to viral clips — so no words go unchecked.

Analyze with AI

Our AI extracts factual claims, matches them against reputable sources, and verifies accuracy in real time — making fact-checking scalable and instant.

Score

Each statement is turned into a transparent credibility score, complete with citations and explanations, so users can quickly see what’s accurate and what’s not.

Deliver

Users get clear, digestible credibility records across time — making it easy to track who can be trusted and hold powerful voices accountable.

What Cred Does

Make credibility measurable. Cred helps you ingest claims, score sources, and surface voices that can be trusted.

Our platform brings fact-checking into your daily workflow: collect statements from social and news, evaluate them with transparent scoring, cite reputable sources, and publish shareable results. Teams use Cred to reduce misinformation and boost trust with their audience.

Claim Ingestion & Parsing

Pull posts, quotes, and clips from social and the web. We auto-detect entities, topics, and time references so each claim is ready for review and scoring.

Credibility Scoring Engine

Weighted, transparent scores backed by citations. Customize rubrics, track revisions over time, and explain the “why” behind every rating.

Source Graph & Citations

Link every claim to reputable sources. Explore relationships between outlets, experts, and topics to spot patterns and conflicts of interest.

Browser & Social Extensions

See Cred scores where you browse. Our lightweight extensions overlay credibility context on tweets, videos, and articles in real time.

Publisher & Community Tools

Embed trust badges, add context panels, and enable community notes with moderation queues, audit trails, and role-based approvals.

Developer API & Integrations

Sync data to Slack, Notion, CMS, and BI tools. Webhooks and a REST API let you trigger reviews, fetch scores, and automate reporting.
